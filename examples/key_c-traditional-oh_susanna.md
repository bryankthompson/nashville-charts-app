NOTES:
  - Written by Stephen Foster (1848), public domain
  - Classic American folk song, 1-4-5 structure
  - Often the first song taught in beginning guitar/banjo classes
  - The chorus shifts tonal center briefly to the 4 (F) before resolving
  - Can be performed with fingerpicking or flatpick strumming
  - Key of C keeps everything in open position — no barres needed
