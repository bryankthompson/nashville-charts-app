NOTES:
  - Traditional African American spiritual, public domain
  - Attributed to Wallace Willis (Choctaw Nation, pre-1862)
  - Simple 1-4-5 progression throughout
  - The song begins with the chorus — teach it first for sing-alongs
  - Key of A is comfortable for most voices and open guitar shapes
  - The 2- (Bm) can be substituted on the final "carry me home" for color
  - Works beautifully as a cappella — the chords are implied by the melody
